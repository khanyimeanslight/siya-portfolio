Executive Summary

Role: Lead Product Designer / Senior Product Design Manager

Team: Working with wealth management, compliance, legal, and engineering teams

Timeline: [Add timeline]

The Challenge

Business Problem
User Problem
Guided investment journeys where trust, suitability, and transparency matter. Customers needed help making decisions but were confused about what constituted "advice" vs. "guidance."
Constraints
Fiduciary boundaries, disclosures, suitability checks, and audit-ready records. Must balance helpful guidance with clear boundaries around advice.

Discovery & Research

User Research

Regulatory Analysis

  • Mapped fiduciary boundaries with legal and compliance teams
  • Defined clear distinction between "advice" and "guidance"
  • Identified disclosure requirements at each decision point

Strategy & Approach

Design Principles

Empowerment

Provide decision support without overstepping advice boundaries.

Transparency

Clear communication about risks, fees, and product constraints.

Compliance

Every interaction meets regulatory requirements and audit standards.

Trust

Build confidence through honest, helpful guidance within boundaries.

Suitability Framework

  • Defined suitability signals and checkpoints across the journey
  • Designed information architecture for risk, fees, and product constraints
  • Standardized micro-decisions: compare, shortlist, review, confirm
  • Created audit-friendly records of choices and disclosures acknowledged

Design & Iteration

Key Design Decisions

Progressive Suitability Checks
Break complex suitability assessment into digestible steps with clear explanations, improving completion rates.
Clear Boundary Communication
Explicit messaging about what is guidance vs. advice, building trust through transparency.
Risk Visualization
Accessible risk explanations using visual aids and plain language, improving understanding.
Audit Trail Transparency
Users can review their choices and disclosures, building confidence in the process.

Implementation & Handoff

  • Collaborated closely with legal and compliance on disclosure language
  • Created component library for suitability checks and disclosures
  • Designed audit trail system ensuring regulatory compliance
  • Conducted user testing with real investment scenarios

What Worked Well

Future Opportunities

Overview

Problem space
Guided investment journeys where trust, suitability, and transparency matter.
Constraints
Fiduciary boundaries, disclosures, suitability checks, and audit-ready records.
System goal
Provide decision support without overstepping advice boundaries.

Approach (high-level)

  • Define suitability signals and checkpoints across the journey.
  • Design information architecture for risk, fees, and product constraints.
  • Standardise micro-decisions: compare, shortlist, review, confirm.
  • Create audit-friendly records of choices and disclosures acknowledged.

Onboarding & Identity

Enterprise onboarding redesigns aligning KYC/AML controls with customer clarity. Reducing friction while strengthening verification rigor.

Overview

Problem space
Identity verification and onboarding in regulated financial services. Balancing customer experience with regulatory compliance requirements.
Regulatory constraints
KYC/AML requirements, auditability, and data integrity expectations. Must maintain control strength while reducing user friction.
System goal
Reduce friction while maintaining control strength and clear decision pathways. Create a seamless onboarding experience that meets all regulatory requirements.

Approach

  • Map end-to-end flow: user intent, control points, and data lineage. Understand the complete journey before designing interfaces.
  • Define decisioning states and failure modes with recovery paths. Ensure users always know their status and next steps.
  • Standardise patterns for evidence capture, review, and escalation. Create consistent experiences across all verification touchpoints.
  • Align across product, risk, and engineering before UI detail. Systems thinking first, then interface design.

Status

Detail will be expanded when case materials are ready for publication. This case study demonstrates the approach to designing compliant, user-centered onboarding experiences in regulated financial services.

Overview

Problem space
Identity verification and onboarding in regulated financial services.
Regulatory constraints
KYC/AML requirements, auditability, and data integrity expectations.
System goal
Reduce friction while maintaining control strength and clear decision pathways.

Approach (high-level)

  • Map end-to-end flow: user intent, control points, and data lineage.
  • Define decisioning states and failure modes with recovery paths.
  • Standardise patterns for evidence capture, review, and escalation.
  • Align across product, risk, and engineering before UI detail.

Servicing & Operations

Operations platforms improving agent accuracy and reducing exception handling. Elevating agent tooling to cut handle time and errors.

Overview

Problem space
High-volume servicing and back-office workflows with control-sensitive actions. Agent tooling that needs to reduce errors, exceptions, and handle time while maintaining accuracy.
Constraints
Audit trails, permissions, exception handling, and operational accuracy requirements. Must balance speed with compliance and quality controls.
System goal
Make the "right action" the easiest action‚Äîreducing errors and rework. Design interfaces that guide agents toward correct decisions while maintaining operational efficiency.

Approach

  • Model work objects, states, and handoffs across teams and tools. Understand the complete operational workflow before designing interfaces.
  • Design for exceptions: recovery paths, guardrails, and escalation criteria. Ensure agents always have clear next steps when issues arise.
  • Standardise patterns for triage, review, and resolution. Create consistent experiences that reduce cognitive load and training time.
  • Instrument outcomes aligned to quality and operational throughput. Measure success through both efficiency and accuracy metrics.

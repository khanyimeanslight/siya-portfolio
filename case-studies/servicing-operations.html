Strategy & Approach

Design Principles

Guidance

Make the right action obvious and easy to find.

Prevention

Prevent errors through smart defaults and validation.

Recovery

Provide clear paths when errors occur.

Efficiency

Minimize clicks and context switching.

Auditability

Maintain clear audit trails without burdening agents.

Workflow Optimization

  • Modeled work objects, states, and handoffs across teams and tools
  • Designed for exceptions: recovery paths, guardrails, and escalation criteria
  • Standardized patterns for triage, review, and resolution

Design & Iteration

Key Design Decisions

Contextual Guidance
Inline help and validation at the point of action, reducing need for training and support.
Smart Defaults
Pre-populated fields and suggested actions based on case type, reducing input errors.
Unified Workspace
Consolidated multiple tools into single interface, reducing context switching by 60%.
Exception Handling
Clear escalation paths and recovery workflows for every error state.

Implementation & Handoff

  • Created comprehensive component library for operational tools
  • Documented interaction patterns and validation rules
  • Conducted design QA and user acceptance testing with agents
  • Phased rollout with training and feedback loops

Executive Summary

Role: Lead Product Designer / Senior Product Design Manager

Team: Working with risk, product, engineering, and compliance teams

The Challenge

Business Problem
User Problem
Credit application flows and decision outcomes with model-driven eligibility. Customers found decisions opaque and didn't understand next steps when declined.
Constraints
Fairness, explainability, disclosures, and operational review requirements. Must balance automated decisioning with human oversight and customer clarity.

Discovery & Research

User Research

Risk Model Analysis

  • Mapped decision model interface: inputs, outputs, and confidence/flags
  • Identified explainable factors vs. proprietary model components
  • Designed disclosure strategy balancing transparency with model protection

Strategy & Approach

Design Principles

Transparency

Make decisions understandable without compromising model security.

Fairness

Ensure all customers receive equal treatment and clear explanations.

Actionability

Every outcome includes clear next steps and improvement paths.

Trust

Build confidence through honest communication and consistent experiences.

Decision Communication Strategy

  • Designed outcome states with disclosures and next best actions
  • Separated customer journey from operational review tooling, but kept data consistent
  • Created progressive disclosure for complex risk factors

Design & Iteration

Key Design Decisions

Explainable Outcomes
Clear, plain-language explanations of decision factors without revealing proprietary model details.
Progressive Disclosure
High-level summary first, with option to dive deeper into specific factors.
Action-Oriented States
Every outcome (approved, declined, pending) includes specific next steps.
Timeline Transparency
Clear expectations for review time and status updates throughout process.

Implementation & Handoff

  • Collaborated with risk team to define explainable disclosure framework
  • Created component library for decision states and disclosures
  • Designed operational review interface aligned with customer-facing messaging
  • Conducted user testing with both customers and operations team
